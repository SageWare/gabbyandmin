2. Information we collect

In the pursuit of improving our services, we collect personal information (such as your name, address, and email) and non-personal information pertaining to you and your use of our services through the following methods:

A. Information provided to us directly by you:
✦ Website Forms and Newsletters – When signing up for newsletters, filling out online forms, making a donation, applying for jobs online, or providing feedback on our services, we may request that you provide personal information to us.
✦ Magazine Subscriptions – To subscribe to the Gabby & Min's Literary Review or other publications through our services, you may need to create an account either with us or one of our Service Providers. Creating an account through G&M's website may require you to provide personal information and payment data, including credit card information. This information could be utilized and stored by G&M and/or the Service Provider to fulfill your subscription, process payments, and offer other products or services that may be relevant to you.
✦ Surveys – Occasionally, G&M and our Service Providers may gather personal information via online surveys, polls, or other interactive activities centered around subjects related to our programs or services. Taking part in these surveys is completely optional.
✦ Contests and Special Offers – When you engage with contests or special offers, G&M obtains personal information like your name and email.

B. Data gathered through your usage of the Services:
✦ Cookies and Other Technologies – Automatically collected information may comprise of usage details (such as the web pages you have viewed on our website or the duration of time spent on a page), IP addresses, and information acquired through browser cookies. A cookie is a tiny piece of data that gets saved on the hard drive of your computer. Activating the appropriate setting on your browser will enable you to refuse browser cookies. However, if you do select this option, you may be unable to access specific areas of the Services. By utilizing the Services with your browser setting adjusted to accept cookies, you approve our use of cookies on the Services. The types of cookies we may use include, but are not limited to:

✧ Google Analytics – Web traffic tracking and analytics. Learn more at https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/, and to opt out, visit http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
✧ Google Tag Manager – Web traffic tracking. Learn more at https://support.google.com/tagmanager/answer/6102821.
✧ Philantro – Facilitation of online donations. Learn more at https://www.philantro.com/cookie-policy.php
✧ Cloudflare – Content delivery and security. Learn more at https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/200170156-What-does-the-Cloudflare-cfduid-cookie-do-

✦ Local Storage Objects and Advertising: Local Storage Objects ("LSOs") such as HTML5 may be utilized by us to save content information and preferences. We also collaborate with Service Providers to offer certain features on our website and Services or to exhibit advertising based on your web browsing activity using LSOs such as HTML5 to gather and retain information. Numerous browsers offer their own management tools for removing HTML5 LSOs.
✦ Behavioral Targeting/Re-Targeting: To show advertisements on our Services or to administer our advertising on other websites, we may employ a third-party ad network such as Facebook Pixel and Google Tag Manager. Our ad network and magazine subscription Service Providers might employ cookies and Web beacons to collect non-personal information regarding your activities on our Services and other websites. This is done to offer targeted advertising based on your interests.
✦ Social Media Widgets: Social media features, such as Facebook's "Like" button, "Share" button, and interactive mini-programs may be included on our Services. These features may collect information such as your IP address and other data, and may use cookies to function properly. These may be hosted either by a third-party or directly on our Services. The privacy policy of the company providing these features governs your interactions with them.
✦ Facebook Custom Audiences: Our participation in Facebook's Custom Audience program allows us to show personalized ads to individuals on our email lists when they visit Facebook. To do this, we provide Facebook with personal information, such as your email address and phone number, which is used to identify whether you have a registered account with Facebook. If you wish to opt-out of Facebook Custom Audience, you can send us an email from the same email address you want to opt-out for.
✦ Do Not Track Signals: At present, we do not take any measures to respond to "Do Not Track" signals as there is no consistent technological standard in place. We keep examining new technologies and may implement a standard when one becomes available.

Gabby & Min's Literary Review, Issue #6 Fall 2024

GABBY & MIN'S LITERARY REVIEW
Issue #6, Fall 2024

Gabby & Min's Literary Review is delighted to present its sixth issue for Fall 2024, embracing the season’s rich tapestry of change and introspection. This captivating edition features eleven stunning artworks that capture the warm, earthy tones and reflective spirit of autumn. Complementing these visual creations are five thought-provoking stories, each exploring themes of transition, nostalgia, and the complexity of relationships. The issue also showcases seven striking photographs, preserving moments that highlight the fleeting splendor of this time of year. To further elevate the collection, sixteen poignant poems and prose pieces delve into themes of loss, renewal, and inner strength. Every piece within this issue, whether visual or literary, embodies the distinctive perspective and talent of its creator. Issue #6 invites readers to immerse themselves in the poignant beauty and depth found within Gabby & Min's Literary Review, celebrating the profound influence of art and storytelling in capturing the essence of autumn.

Featuring:

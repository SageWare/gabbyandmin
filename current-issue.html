Gabby & Min's Literary Review, Issue #10 Fall 2025

GABBY & MIN'S LITERARY REVIEW
Issue #10, Fall 2025

Gabby & Min’s Literary Review is honored to present its milestone Issue #10, a Fall Edition that lingers in the quiet flame of the season—where time stretches, light thins, and change arrives with a soft but certain clarity. Rooted in reflection and “intentional slowness,” this tenth issue moves through haunted rooms, fractured memories, and tender thresholds, inviting readers to pause inside each moment rather than rush past it. Across six evocative artworks, four works of fiction, ten poems and prose pieces, and seven photographs, contributors explore grief and survival, family and distance, ritual and rebirth—always with an eye toward the small embers of beauty that refuse to go out. From late-night taxi rides and autumn roads to witch-sister spells and iced-over ponds, Issue #10 gathers work that burns low but steady, illuminating the strange, luminous edges of fall and the stories we carry forward.

Featuring:
Abby Pullan, Alan Kim, Ashley Patrice, Ayshe Dengtash, CLS Sandoval, Fatemeh Mousavi, Frances Fish, Gael Blake, Gregory O’Neill, Isabella Suell, Joel M. Scinta, John Brady, Joshua Adam Walker, Julie Epp, Kenneth Pobo, Kyler Akagi, Larissa Monique Hauck, Lawrence Bridges, Liana Meyer, Matthew McCain, McKenna Wilds, Meg Taylor, Nancy Santos, Robin Young, Rory Brouillard, Suwan Choi, Teresa Blake

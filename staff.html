Meet The Staff

Sage Delio might be considered a modern day renaissance woman, with her diverse interests and talents spanning across the fields of creative writing, computer science, music, and the arts. In May 2022, she published her debut poetry collection, Blue Confessional: Poetry and Prose. A second edition of the collection is being adapted with Sage’s own art and illustrations. For Gabby & Min’s Literary Review, Sage holds the roles of Editor-In-Chief and Poetry & Prose Editor. As Editor-In-Chief, she defines the publication's vision, oversees the editorial team, creates editorial policies and guidelines, and ensures high-quality standards are met. As the Poetry & Prose Editor, Sage reviews and edits the poetry and prose submissions and makes sure they align to the publication's vision.

Sage Delio

Editor-In-Chief
Poetry & Prose Editor

Sharon Fremont is an artist and avid book enthusiast, with a passion for exploring a range of art forms; including, watercolor painting and sketching. For Gabby & Min’s Literary Review, Sharon holds dual roles as Managing Editor and Fiction Editor. In her role as Managing Editor, she oversees the day-to-day operations, provides guidance and feedback to editorial staff, and hires and manages new staff members. As Fiction Editor, she reviews and edits fiction submissions, ensures that the content aligns with the publication's principles, and collaborates with the editorial team to maintain high standards. Sharon’s knowledge and commitment to excellence helps to improve the overall content of the publication.

Sharon Fremont

Managing Editor
Fiction Editor

Karen Porterfield has spent over 24 years working in design. She has established herself as a talented artisan jewelry designer, crafting one-of-a-kind pieces that are highly sought after. Karen's passion for creating beautiful and innovative designs has led her to achieve a great deal of success in the field. She serves as the Art & Illustrations Editor for Gabby & Min's Literary Review. In this role, she is responsible for reviewing any art and illustration submissions and selecting the pieces that best uphold the publication’s motives. Karen's keen eye for detail makes her an essential part of the publication's team.

Karen Porterfield

Art & Illustrations Editor

Matthew Evan is an accomplished photographer and passionate car enthusiast. He has developed a sharp eye for capturing the beauty and essence of his subjects through his lens. He joins Gabby & Min’s Literary Review as the publication’s Photography Editor. As Photography Editor, Matthew is responsible for selecting and curating the photographic submissions of the publication and ensuring that it aligns with the overall goals and expectations of the review. His expertise and dedication to his craft make him an invaluable asset to the team.  

Matthew Evan

Photography Editor

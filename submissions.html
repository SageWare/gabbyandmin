Before Submitting to the following Open Calls, please read the Submission Guidelines.

INTERNATIONAL OPEN CALL FOR POETRY & PROSE

Each single poetry/prose must be up to two typed pages max.
Please submit up to 3 poetry/prose in one document.

SUBMIT

INTERNATIONAL OPEN CALL FOR FICTION

Please Proofread your work. We must decline pieces that need major editing, even if they are otherwise good.
There is no minimum word count, but please keep your Fiction Submission under 3000 words.
Please submit 1 Fiction Submission per one document.

SUBMIT

INTERNATIONAL OPEN CALL FOR ART & ILLUSTRATIONS

You can submit up to 2 images per submission.
The Gabby & Min: Literary Review has the dimensions 8.5" x 11", with no framed margins, so submit your artwork or illustration accordingly. If you do not want your submission to be "cut off," please include such margins or a border in your image file.

SUBMIT

INTERNATIONAL OPEN CALL FOR PHOTOGAPHY

 You can submit up to 2 photographs per submission.
The Gabby & Min: Literary Review has the dimensions 8.5" x 11", with no framed margins, so submit your photographs accordingly. If you do not want your submission to be "cut off," please include such margins or a border in your image file.
